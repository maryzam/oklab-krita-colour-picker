# Usage

Open **Settings > Dockers > OKLab Colour Selector** and place the docker where
it fits your workspace.

## Selector Modes

- **Hue/Chroma**: choose hue and chroma at the current lightness.
- **Hue/Lightness**: choose hue and lightness at the current chroma.
- **Lightness/Chroma**: choose lightness and chroma at the current hue.
- **Hue Ring**: choose hue on a ring, with lightness and chroma controls in
  the center.

## Controls

- Drag inside a selector to preview and commit a colour.
- Use the L, C, and H gradient sliders for direct OKLCh adjustment.
- Use numeric inputs for precise lightness, chroma, and hue values.
- Enter a six-digit hex colour with or without `#`.
- Use the current and previous swatches to compare recent selections.

Selections are constrained to the visible sRGB gamut, so committed colours
match what the selector can display.
